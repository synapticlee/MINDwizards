You are a wizard's apprentice, learning to buy magical gems for the wizard. The wizard is training you by letting you guess the value of different gems and then telling you the real value.

Through his feedback on your guesses you must learn how to quickly and accurately estimate the value of new gems on your own when the wizard isn't around to help.

Here's an example of what your task looks like:

The size of the bars on the left side represent the level of different kinds of magic that each gem possesses. The size of these magic power bars goes into determining the gem's value represented by the size of the bar on the right.

It's important to note that the wizard does not necessarily care about all kinds of magic equally. Some bars may be more important others.

You start by guessing a value by clicking on the value bar on the right with your mouse.

The wizard will give you feedback so you can learn how to value gems. You must use the wizard's feedback to figure out which magic powers he cares about most and least. Then you can use what you've learned to help when he isn't around.

Try your best to learn what makes a great wizard gem! When you're ready to begin, press the "Begin" button below!
